🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Introduction to Mental Models

🔍 What is it?
Mental models are simplified frameworks we use to understand the world, make decisions, and solve problems more effectively. Think of them as the lenses through which we interpret everything around us.

🛠️ How to Apply It
Build a diverse toolkit of models drawn from multiple disciplines, psychology, economics, physics, biology. Apply the most relevant model to the situation at hand, rather than forcing one model onto every problem.

💡 Try This Today
Pick one new mental model each week, study it, and consciously look for one real situation where it applies. Small habit. Big results over time. 🎯
