🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: The Map is Not the Territory

🔍 What is it?
Any model, plan, or description of reality is a simplification, the real world always contains more detail and surprise than the map suggests. Don't confuse the menu for the meal. 🗺️

🛠️ How to Apply It
Treat your plans and mental models as useful approximations, not ground truth. Actively seek out what your model omits. Update your map quickly when reality contradicts it, rather than defending the map.

💡 Try This Today
Identify one assumption in a current plan and go verify it directly in the real world this week. What does the territory actually look like? 🎯
