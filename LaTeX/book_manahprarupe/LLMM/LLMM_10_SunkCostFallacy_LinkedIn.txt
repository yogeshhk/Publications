🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Sunk Cost Fallacy

🔍 What is it?
Past investment, time, money, or effort already spent, is gone regardless of what you do next and should never drive future decisions. Don't let yesterday's cost steal tomorrow's opportunity. 🚪

🛠️ How to Apply It
Separate what you have spent from what you stand to gain going forward. Ask: "If I had not already invested in this, would I choose to start it today?" If no, stop.

💡 Try This Today
List one project you are continuing mainly because of what you have already put in, then honestly evaluate its future value, not its past cost. 🎯
