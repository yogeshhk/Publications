🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Systems Thinking

🔍 What is it?
See the world as interconnected systems with feedback loops rather than as isolated chains of cause and effect. Every action ripples through a web of relationships. 🕸️

🛠️ How to Apply It
Map the key elements of a problem and draw the relationships between them, including feedback loops. Look for delays, reinforcing loops that amplify, and balancing loops that dampen, then intervene at high-leverage points.

💡 Try This Today
Draw a simple diagram of your biggest current problem showing at least three elements and how they influence each other. See what the map reveals. 🎯
