🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: The Availability Heuristic

🔍 What is it?
We judge the likelihood of events by how easily examples come to mind, causing vivid or recent events to feel far more probable than they actually are. Memory is not a reliable statistician. 🧠

🛠️ How to Apply It
When assessing risk or frequency, deliberately seek out base-rate statistics rather than relying on memorable anecdotes. Ask: "What does the data say?" before asking: "What do I recall?"

💡 Try This Today
Identify one risk you worry about disproportionately, then look up its actual statistical probability. The numbers may surprise you. 🎯
