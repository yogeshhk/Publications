🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: The Law of Diminishing Returns

🔍 What is it?
Beyond a certain point, adding more input yields progressively smaller increases in output, and eventually none at all. More effort is not always better effort. 📉

🛠️ How to Apply It
Find the inflection point in any effort, the moment when additional time, money, or resources stop yielding meaningful gains. Reallocate resources to higher-return activities before that point, rather than pushing past it.

💡 Try This Today
Identify one area where you are investing heavily and honestly ask: is the last 20% of effort producing meaningful results? If not, redirect it. 🎯
