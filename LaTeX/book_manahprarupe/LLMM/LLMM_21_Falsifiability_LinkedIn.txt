🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Falsifiability

🔍 What is it?
A claim is only scientifically meaningful if it is possible, in principle, to prove it wrong, unfalsifiable beliefs are beyond the reach of evidence. If nothing could disprove it, it is not knowledge. 🔬

🛠️ How to Apply It
For any belief or hypothesis, ask: "What evidence would convince me I am wrong?" If no such evidence exists, treat the belief as a matter of faith rather than fact.

💡 Try This Today
Pick one strongly held belief and define the specific observation that would cause you to abandon it. If you can't name one, that's important information. 🎯
