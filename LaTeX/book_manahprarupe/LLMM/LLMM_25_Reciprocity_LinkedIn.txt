🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Reciprocity

🔍 What is it?
People feel a powerful obligation to return favours, gifts, and concessions, giving first creates a natural pull toward return. Generosity is not just kind; it is strategically wise. 🤝

🛠️ How to Apply It
Build relationships by giving value first without expectation. In negotiation, make a concession and the other party will feel compelled to reciprocate. Be aware that reciprocity can also be weaponised through unsolicited gifts.

💡 Try This Today
Offer genuine help to one person in your network this week, with no immediate ask attached. Watch what returns over time. 🎯
