🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Thought Experiment

🔍 What is it?
Explore hypothetical scenarios in the mind to test principles, stress-test beliefs, or generate insight, without real-world experimentation. The laboratory is your imagination. 💭

🛠️ How to Apply It
Construct a simplified imaginary scenario that isolates the key variable you want to understand. Reason through the scenario rigorously. Use the conclusion to inform your real-world view or decision.

💡 Try This Today
Ask yourself: "What would happen if I doubled my price / halved my team / removed this feature?", and reason it through completely before dismissing the idea. 🎯
