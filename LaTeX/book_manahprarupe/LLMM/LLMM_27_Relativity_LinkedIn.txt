🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Relativity

🔍 What is it?
Observations of speed, position, time, and many human judgments are not absolute, they depend entirely on the observer's frame of reference. Perspective shapes reality. 🔭

🛠️ How to Apply It
When evaluating a situation, identify what frame of reference you are using and how it differs from others involved. Deliberately adopt another vantage point to test whether your conclusions hold.

💡 Try This Today
In a current disagreement, write a full description of the situation from the other party's perspective, before responding. Then see if your response changes. 🎯
