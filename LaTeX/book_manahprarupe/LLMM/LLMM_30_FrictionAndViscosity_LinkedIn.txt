🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Friction and Viscosity

🔍 What is it?
Resistance embedded in a system, whether physical, procedural, or psychological, slows the flow of effort and output. Friction is the hidden tax on everything you do. ⚙️

🛠️ How to Apply It
Map the friction points in any process: unnecessary approvals, unclear steps, tool-switching costs. Eliminate or reduce each one. Conversely, add deliberate friction to behaviours you want to discourage.

💡 Try This Today
Count the steps in one recurring process and remove at least one step that adds no value. Even small friction removal compounds over time. 🎯
