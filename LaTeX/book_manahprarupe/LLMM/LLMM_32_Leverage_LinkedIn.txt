🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Leverage

🔍 What is it?
A small, well-placed force applied at the right point can move a load far beyond what direct effort alone could achieve. The right lever changes everything. 🏗️

🛠️ How to Apply It
Find the highest-leverage point in any system, a key relationship, a bottleneck process, a critical assumption. Apply disproportionate effort or resources there rather than distributing it evenly.

💡 Try This Today
List your five current activities and mark the one that unlocks the most value for others, then invest there first tomorrow. That is your lever. 🎯
