🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Replication

🔍 What is it?
The ability to copy a successful pattern faithfully, and the recognition that imperfect copying introduces variation that can drive improvement. Scale begins with replication. 🔁

🛠️ How to Apply It
Document successful processes so they can be replicated consistently. Allow enough variation in replication to surface improvements. Distinguish between errors (fix them) and productive mutations (explore them).

💡 Try This Today
Document one process you do repeatedly, so precisely that someone else could replicate it with zero guidance from you. If you can't, it is not yet scalable. 🎯
