🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Ecosystems

🔍 What is it?
A community of interdependent elements, organisms, organisations, or ideas, that sustain each other through flows of energy, information, or value. No node survives alone. 🌿

🛠️ How to Apply It
Map the ecosystem around your product, team, or community: who produces, who consumes, who decomposes and recycles. Design for the health of the whole system, not just your node within it.

💡 Try This Today
Draw a simple map of five entities that your work depends on, and identify the most fragile link in that web. Then strengthen it. 🎯
