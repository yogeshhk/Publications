🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Niches

🔍 What is it?
In any ecosystem, lasting success comes from finding and occupying a distinct role that others are not filling, not from competing head-on for the same resources. Depth beats breadth. 🎯

🛠️ How to Apply It
Define the specific, narrow problem you solve better than anyone else. Resist pressure to compete in every dimension. Depth in a niche creates a durable advantage that broad competition erodes.

💡 Try This Today
Write a one-sentence description of the precise niche your work occupies, if it sounds like everyone else, sharpen it until it is unmistakably yours. 🎯
