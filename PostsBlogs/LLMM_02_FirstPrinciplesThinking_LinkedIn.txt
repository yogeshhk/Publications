🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: First Principles Thinking

🔍 What is it?
Break a problem down to its most fundamental, indisputable truths, then reason upward from scratch rather than relying on analogy or convention.

🛠️ How to Apply It
List every assumption you hold about the problem. Challenge each one: "Is this definitely true?" Discard inherited wisdom that cannot be verified, and rebuild your solution only from what survives.

💡 Try This Today
Take a belief you hold about your work or life and ask "Why?" five times in a row, until you hit bedrock fact. You may be surprised what doesn't survive the test. 🎯
