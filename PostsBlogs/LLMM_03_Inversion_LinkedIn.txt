🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Inversion

🔍 What is it?
Instead of asking how to achieve a goal, ask what would guarantee failure, then avoid those things. Sometimes the clearest path forward is found by thinking backwards. 🔄

🛠️ How to Apply It
Define the desired outcome clearly. Brainstorm every action, habit, or condition that would guarantee you never reach it. Eliminate or guard against those failure modes rather than only chasing success.

💡 Try This Today
Before your next project kick-off, write a "pre-mortem": assume it failed completely and list all the causes. Then work backwards to remove them before you even begin. 🎯
