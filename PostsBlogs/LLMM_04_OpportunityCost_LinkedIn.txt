🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Opportunity Cost

🔍 What is it?
Every choice you make implicitly rejects every alternative, the value of the best foregone alternative is your true cost. Every "yes" is also a "no" to something else. ⚖️

🛠️ How to Apply It
Before committing to any decision, explicitly name the next-best alternative. Weigh the value of what you gain against the value of what you give up, not just the monetary or time price.

💡 Try This Today
Before your next big "yes," write down the single best thing you are saying "no" to, and decide consciously. Awareness is the first step. 🎯
