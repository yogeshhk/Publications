🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Second-Order Thinking

🔍 What is it?
Think beyond the immediate consequence of a decision to the downstream effects it will trigger in turn. Most people stop at the first effect, great thinkers go deeper. 🌊

🛠️ How to Apply It
Map your decision three steps deep: "If I do X, then Y happens, and if Y happens, then Z follows." Evaluate whether Z, not just Y, is what you actually want.

💡 Try This Today
For your next important decision, write out the full chain: Action → 1st effect → 2nd effect → 3rd effect. What emerges at the end might surprise you. 🎯
