🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Circle of Competence

🔍 What is it?
Stick to decisions within the domain you genuinely understand deeply, and know the boundary of your expertise. Knowing what you don't know is a superpower. 🎯

🛠️ How to Apply It
Draw three honest circles: what you know deeply (operate here), what you are learning (expand carefully), what is outside your knowledge (defer or avoid). Before taking action, ask: which circle is this in?

💡 Try This Today
Write down your three genuine areas of deep expertise and explicitly label your next big decision as inside or outside them. Honesty here is everything. 🎯
