🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: The Lindy Effect

🔍 What is it?
The longer something non-perishable has already survived, the longer it is likely to continue to survive in the future. Time is the ultimate test of durability. ⏳

🛠️ How to Apply It
When evaluating ideas, technologies, or practices, favour those with long track records over new entrants, especially in high-stakes domains. A book read for 500 years will likely be read for 500 more.

💡 Try This Today
Before adopting a new tool or methodology, ask how old the underlying principle is, not just how new the product looks. Longevity signals robustness. 🎯
