🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: The Pareto Principle (80/20 Rule)

🔍 What is it?
Roughly 80% of outcomes come from 20% of causes, a small number of inputs drive the vast majority of results. Work smarter, not just harder. 📊

🛠️ How to Apply It
Track your activities for one week and identify which 20% produce 80% of your meaningful results. Ruthlessly protect time for those activities and minimise or delegate the rest.

💡 Try This Today
List your top 10 tasks and circle the two that drive most of your results, then schedule those first thing tomorrow. 🎯
