🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Cognitive Biases

🔍 What is it?
Cognitive biases are systematic errors in thinking that cause us to deviate from rational judgment, usually without any awareness that it is happening. 🧠

🛠️ How to Apply It
Learn the most common biases (anchoring, availability, confirmation, etc.) and build check-in questions into important decisions. Slow down deliberate thinking when the stakes are high.

💡 Try This Today
Before your next major decision, name the one bias most likely to distort your thinking, and design a simple counter-check to neutralise it. 🎯
