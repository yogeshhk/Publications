🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Probabilistic Thinking

🔍 What is it?
Think in likelihoods and ranges rather than certainties, assign probabilities to outcomes rather than treating one scenario as inevitable. Uncertainty is not the enemy; ignoring it is. 🎲

🛠️ How to Apply It
For any forecast, identify the best case, worst case, and most likely case with rough probability weights. Update your probabilities as new evidence arrives rather than locking in an early view.

💡 Try This Today
For your next big decision, explicitly write: "I think there is a __% chance this works, __% it partially works, __% it fails." Make uncertainty visible. 🎯
