🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: The Dunning-Kruger Effect

🔍 What is it?
People with limited knowledge in a domain tend to overestimate their competence, while true experts tend to underestimate theirs. The less we know, the more confident we often feel. 📉📈

🛠️ How to Apply It
Be most sceptical of your own confidence when you are new to a subject. Seek expert review of your work. Interpret high certainty as a signal to double-check, not a green light to proceed.

💡 Try This Today
In a domain where you feel very confident, find one expert who disagrees with you, and genuinely try to understand their view before dismissing it. 🎯
