🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Hanlon's Razor

🔍 What is it?
Never attribute to malice what can be adequately explained by incompetence, ignorance, or accident. Most friction in life is not intentional, and assuming otherwise is costly. 🤝

🛠️ How to Apply It
When someone's action harms or frustrates you, consider the most charitable explanation first. Ask whether the person had the knowledge, intention, and motive to act maliciously before concluding they did.

💡 Try This Today
The next time you feel wronged, write two alternative explanations, one malicious, one accidental, and investigate before deciding which fits. 🎯
