🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Skin in the Game

🔍 What is it?
Decision-makers should bear real consequences, upside and downside, for the outcomes of their choices. Advice without accountability is noise. 🎯

🛠️ How to Apply It
Align incentives so that the people making recommendations also share in the results. When seeking advice, prefer those who have personal stakes in the outcome over pure observers.

💡 Try This Today
Before taking advice from someone, ask: "What does this person lose if they are wrong?" Let the answer inform how much weight you give their words. 🎯
