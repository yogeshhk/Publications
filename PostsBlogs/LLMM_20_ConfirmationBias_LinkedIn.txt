🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Confirmation Bias

🔍 What is it?
We unconsciously seek, interpret, and remember information that confirms what we already believe, while quietly discarding what challenges us. Our minds are naturally self-defending. 🪞

🛠️ How to Apply It
Actively search for evidence that contradicts your current view. Assign a "devil's advocate" role in group decisions. Weight disconfirming data at least as heavily as confirming data.

💡 Try This Today
Read one article or talk to one person who holds the opposite view to yours on an important topic, before finalising your position. 🎯
