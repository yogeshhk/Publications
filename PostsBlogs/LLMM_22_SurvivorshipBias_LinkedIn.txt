🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Survivorship Bias

🔍 What is it?
We draw conclusions from visible successes while ignoring the invisible failures that never made it through the filter. We study the winners and forget the graveyard. 🏆

🛠️ How to Apply It
Whenever you study success stories, actively ask what happened to those who tried the same approach and failed. Look for the graveyard of failed attempts before modelling your strategy on the survivors.

💡 Try This Today
Find one success story you admire and research the failure rate of others who followed the same path. Let the full picture inform your decisions. 🎯
