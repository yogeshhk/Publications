🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: The Peter Principle

🔍 What is it?
In hierarchies, people are promoted based on performance in their current role, until they reach a level of incompetence where they stay. Excellence in one role does not guarantee excellence in the next. 📊

🛠️ How to Apply It
Promote people based on the skills required in the new role, not just their performance in the old one. Recognise that an excellent individual contributor may not be an effective manager.

💡 Try This Today
Evaluate your next promotion decision on the requirements of the destination role, not the track record in the current one. Ask: does this person have what the new role needs? 🎯
