🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Tragedy of the Commons

🔍 What is it?
Shared resources are depleted when individuals, acting in their own rational self-interest, collectively over-consume them. What is good for each person can be catastrophic for everyone. 🌍

🛠️ How to Apply It
Identify any shared resource, attention, budget, infrastructure, where individual incentives conflict with collective welfare. Design governance rules, quotas, or ownership structures to align individual and group interests.

💡 Try This Today
Spot one shared resource in your team or organisation that is being over-used, and propose one constraint or governance rule to protect it. 🎯
