🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Thermodynamics

🔍 What is it?
Closed systems naturally drift toward disorder (entropy) over time, maintaining order requires continuous energy input. Left alone, everything tends toward chaos. 🌡️

🛠️ How to Apply It
Apply this to organisations, relationships, and projects: without deliberate maintenance and investment, they degrade. Build regular "energy inputs", reviews, re-alignment sessions, maintenance cycles, into every system you run.

💡 Try This Today
Identify one system in your life or work that is drifting toward disorder, and schedule a deliberate maintenance action this week. 🎯
