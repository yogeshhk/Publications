🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Inertia

🔍 What is it?
Objects, and organisations, habits, and people, tend to remain in their current state until acted upon by a sufficient external force. Change requires overcoming resistance, not just wanting it. 🏋️

🛠️ How to Apply It
To create change, apply enough concentrated force to overcome initial resistance rather than spreading effort thinly. To prevent unwanted change, build in stabilising structures that resist outside disruption.

💡 Try This Today
Identify one habit you want to change and design one specific, high-force trigger to overcome the inertia of the current state. Make the push undeniable. 🎯
