🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Velocity

🔍 What is it?
Unlike speed, velocity includes direction, moving fast in the wrong direction is worse than moving slowly in the right one. Busyness without direction is not progress. 🧭

🛠️ How to Apply It
Define both the rate of progress and the direction before optimising for speed. Regularly confirm that your fast-moving efforts are still pointed at the right goal, adjust direction before accelerating further.

💡 Try This Today
Ask of your current most urgent project: "Are we going fast in the right direction, or just going fast?" The answer matters more than the speed. 🎯
