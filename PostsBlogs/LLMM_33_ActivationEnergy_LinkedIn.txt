🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Activation Energy

🔍 What is it?
Every process requires an initial energy input to get started, lower that barrier and the reaction proceeds naturally. The hardest part is almost always just beginning. ⚡

🛠️ How to Apply It
Identify what makes starting hard, ambiguity, setup cost, emotional resistance, and reduce it. Make the first step trivially easy. In habit formation, design the environment so the activation cost of the desired behaviour is near zero.

💡 Try This Today
Reduce the first step of one important habit to under two minutes, so it requires almost no activation energy to begin. Then begin. 🎯
