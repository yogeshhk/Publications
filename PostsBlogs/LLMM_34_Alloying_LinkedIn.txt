🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Alloying

🔍 What is it?
Combining two or more different materials or disciplines creates something stronger than any single element alone. The best ideas, teams, and products are rarely pure, they are alloys. 🔩

🛠️ How to Apply It
Deliberately mix expertise, perspectives, or resources from different domains. The strongest teams, products, and ideas often emerge from cross-disciplinary combinations that create properties neither element possesses individually.

💡 Try This Today
Bring one person from a completely different field into your next brainstorm, and listen for how their framing changes your thinking. 🎯
