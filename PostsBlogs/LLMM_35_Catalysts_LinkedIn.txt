🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Catalysts

🔍 What is it?
A catalyst accelerates a process without being consumed, it lowers the barrier to reaction without changing the start or end state. Some people, tools, and ideas make everything else move faster. 🚀

🛠️ How to Apply It
Look for people, tools, or conditions that accelerate progress without requiring proportional ongoing effort. Invest in catalytic elements, the right introduction, the right software, the right framing, that make everything else flow faster.

💡 Try This Today
Identify one bottleneck in a current project and ask: "What one person, tool, or change would make this much easier without me carrying it?" Find that catalyst. 🎯
