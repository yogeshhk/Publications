🧠 Let's Learn Mental Models: Evolutionary Natural Selection

🔍 What is it?
Variation plus differential survival equals adaptation, entities best suited to their environment reproduce and persist while others fade. Nature's greatest innovation engine. 🧬

🛠️ How to Apply It
Run many small experiments (variation), measure results honestly (selection), and double down on what survives. Apply this to products, strategies, and habits: create options, test, keep what thrives, discard what doesn't.

💡 Try This Today
Run three small, low-cost experiments on a current challenge this month, and commit to killing the two that perform worst. Let the best survive. 🎯
