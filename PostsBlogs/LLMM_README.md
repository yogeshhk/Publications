## How to Post on LinkedIn

1. Check the table below for today's post number and date.
2. Open the corresponding `_LinkedIn.txt` file — copy its entire content.
3. Go to LinkedIn and click **Start a post**.
4. Paste the copied text into the post composer.
5. Click the image icon and attach the corresponding `_diagrams_N_ai.png` file.
6. Preview the post — verify the hashtags, Amazon and Sakal Publications links, and the @mentions are intact.
7. Click **Post** (or use LinkedIn's scheduling feature to queue it for the exact date and time).
