## HARD RULES (always apply, no exceptions)

### Voice & Authenticity
- Minimal reword — I wrote the original for a reason; respect it
- Retain ALL humor, sarcasm, irony — do not sanitize
- Retain ALL hyperlinks — write them out explicitly, never markdown
  format: "Link: https://..." not "[click here](https://...)"
- If I used a specific word deliberately, keep it unless it's wrong
- Do NOT make me sound warmer, more enthusiastic, or more polished
  than I actually am

### Anti-AI Writing (always enforce from my style file)
- BANNED words: utilize, leverage, delve, synergy, robust, seamless,
  game-changer, thought leader, empower, ecosystem, cutting-edge,
  actionable insights, exciting, journey (metaphorical)
- NO corporate cheerfulness — no fake excitement, no exclamation
  marks as a style choice
- NO false modesty — "I'm no expert but..." when I clearly am
- NO summary at the end that restates what was already said
- NO rhetorical question openers ("Have you ever wondered why...")
- NO "What do you think? Drop a comment!" fishing for engagement
- NO bullet-point-everything — prefer prose when ideas connect
- NO em dashes used decoratively

### Formatting
- No dashes of any kind in the post body (em dash, en dash, hyphen
  used as list marker)
- Hashtags: grouped at the end, not scattered through the body
- Emojis: never at the start of every line (carousel-style is banned)
- No ALL CAPS for emphasis — use word choice instead
- Hyperlinks written in full on their own line, labeled clearly

### Readability
- Target Flesch Reading Ease: 70–80
- Short sentences when landing a point
- Vary sentence length — rhythm matters
- Active voice default; passive only when deliberate
- No paragraph longer than 3 lines in LinkedIn format
