# The One Prompt
## The Single Prompt That Starts 80% of My Conversations with Claude

---

## THE PROMPT

```
I want to [TASK] so that [SUCCESS CRITERIA].

Before you start:
- Ask me 3–5 clarifying questions using a clickable form
- Show me your proposed approach before executing
- Wait for my go-ahead

Do not write a single word of output until I approve the plan.
```

---

## HOW TO USE IT

Replace the bracketed parts:

| Placeholder | Example |
|---|---|
| `[TASK]` | write a LinkedIn post about MidcurveNN |
| `[SUCCESS CRITERIA]` | it sounds like me, teaches something real, gets engineers to think |

**Full example:**
> I want to write a LinkedIn post explaining why most AI bootcamps fail their students so that working engineers see me as a trusted, honest voice — not a course-seller. First, read my folder. Then ask me questions using a clickable form. Show me your plan. Wait for my approval before writing anything.
