You have a song! That's great!

  1. Send an email letting us know which songs you would like to send. Depending on whether someone is, or not already working on them, we will send you the go ahead.

  2. Please use Itrans baraakhaDii: 
    Vowels (dependent and independent):
-------
a      aa/A    i      ii/I     u       uu/U 
RRi/R^i    RRI/R^I       LLi/L^i    LLI/L^I
e      ai        o      au         aM      aH

Example: aaii or AI

Consonants:
----------- 
k     kh     g     gh     ~N
ch    Ch     j     jh     ~n
T     Th     D     Dh     N
t     th     d     dh     n
p     ph     b     bh     m
y     r      l     v / w
sh    Sh     s     h      L
x / kSh     GY / j~n / dny     shr
R (for marathi half-RA)
L / ld (marathi LLA)

Example: aaii laaDU sa.npale!

Specials/Accents:
-----------------
Anusvara:       .n / M / .m  (dot on top of previous consonant/vowel)
Avagraha:       .a    (`S' like symbol basically to replace a after o)
Ardhachandra:   .c    (for vowel sound as in english words `cat' or `talk')
Chandra-Bindu:  .N    (chandra-bindu on top of previous letter)
Halant:         .h    (to get half-form of the consonant - no vowel - virama)
Visarga:        H     (visarga - looks like a colon character)
Om:             OM, AUM (Om symbol)
