Jump directly to a specific song number
If you already know the ITRANS Song Book number of some specific song you are looking for, just enter the number in the box below and click on "Go".
directly to song number in format.
Song numbers range from 1 to 1299. You can also enter a number between 8000 and 8258 or between 9000 and 9219 to get "new" submissions to the ISB that haven't made it into the official release yet.


If you cannot find a song ...
If you want lyrics of a specific song, and you can't find that song in the archives try posting a request on the rec.music.indian.misc newsgroup, and hopefully somebody will post the lyrics you want. If you want the lyrics to old songs, Satish Kalra knows the lyrics of a lot of songs. Send him a request by email and he might send you the lyrics. Please remember, he is doing this as a favor, so he might not always have the time to be able to satisfy your requests. Note that the "latest submissions" to the ISB (from Rajiv Shridhar's site) have now been incorporated into the indexes at this site. (Thanks Rajiv!)

Anurag Shankar's ITRANS SongBook site
The original ITRANS SongBook site. Among other things, gives you the ability to search the hindi songs archive by song title, singer, music director, movie name or lyricist. Also has a bunch of stuff other than hindi songs. This site is mirrored at Sridhar Natarajan's and Rajiv Sridhar's site.
